- SIDE_EXP = S + 2*collar + 1 = 271. If tile=257 points, then 257 + 14 = 271 (natural). If tile=256 points, the +1 is unexplained.
- Adjacent tiles must share boundary: no gap between tiles. Right face of tile A = left face of tile B.
